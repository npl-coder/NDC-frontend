Where Passion Meets Possibility

Introduction

At NPLCoder | Nepal Data Challenge, we believe that the future of technology in Nepal lies in our collective effort to grow, support, and uplift one another. This platform is designed to promote a collaborative, inclusive, and mentorship-driven community of university students who aspire to achieve excellence in AI and Machine Learning.

Through mentorship, collaboration, and a commitment to helping each other, we aim to create lifelong professional networks and foster an environment where all members can thrive—regardless of their background or location. By working together, we can unlock not just individual potential but collective success. Join us in building a supportive ecosystem that will nurture the next generation of leaders and innovators in Nepal.

Key Features

  • Mentorship and Role Models: Gain access to experienced mentors who will guide you throughout the competition.
  • Collaborative Community: Join a network of like-minded individuals who believe in helping each other succeed.
  • Professional Growth: Build lasting professional relationships and tap into resources that can help you with jobs and graduate school opportunities.
  • Inclusivity and Support: We value collaboration over competition, encouraging participants to uplift one another for collective success.
  • Exciting Prizes: Compete for a share of $1,000 in prize money and recognition as a leader in AI/ML.

Rules and Guidelines

Competition Ethos: NPLCoder | Nepal Data Challenge is not just about competing; it's about collaborating and uplifting one another. Our community is built on the principles of mentorship, inclusivity, and long-term professional growth. Every participant is expected to embody these values and contribute to the collective success of the group.

Prize Distribution

  • 1st Place: $500
  • 2nd Place: $300
  • 3rd Place: $200

Eligibility and Team Formation

All university students in Nepal are eligible. Teams can consist of up to 4 members, and cross-discipline teams are encouraged to foster diversity in skills and backgrounds. Participants may register individually or in teams. For individuals, team formation sessions will be provided to ensure everyone finds a team.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Technical Excellence: The quality and accuracy of solutions.
  • Innovation and Creativity: How well participants think outside the box.
  • Collaboration and Support: Teams will be recognized not only for their competitive performance but for how they support others, align with the ethos of mentorship, and contribute to the community’s growth.

Call to Action
